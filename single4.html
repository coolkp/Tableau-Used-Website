Trendline of Acreage consumed by wildfires over years 2016-2018

  • Tableau based analysis


This line graph shows that 2017 was the worst when it comes to damage caused by wildfires. This is supported by the map of fires in 2017 which showed fires occuring regions with threat level 2 and 1. Record temperatures and high velocity winds in some regions during winter in 2017 can be partially blamed for this. Worrylingly, wildfires in 2018 have already consumed as many acres as in 2016.
